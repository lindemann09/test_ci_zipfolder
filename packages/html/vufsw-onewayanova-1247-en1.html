    Question

    A researcher performs a regression analysis using SPSS and receives the following ANOVA table:

    How much variance of Y is explained by this regression model?


    1. FALSE: 4.2%
    2. FALSE: 4.8%
    3. TRUE: 5.1%
    4. FALSE: This can not be calculated on the basis of the above output.

    Solution

    You can calculate the R2 by (total sum of squares -residual) / total sum of squares=(2564,601-2433,288)/ 2564,601= 131,313/2564,601=0,051
    Thus 5,1 %
